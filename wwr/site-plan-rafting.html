Overview

Purpose

Breaking the monotonous routine of everyday life is what we all want. With this in mind, our company Acqua Benders provides the most exciting water experience you could have, navigating the coolest rivers and enjoying a wonderful view of nature. If you like water, getting wet and enjoying family experiences, laughing and having fun, we will take you to a new level of fun.

Audience

Attention extreme sports lovers and families who want different fun. Yes, we have 2 types of Rafting! For those who already have experience and want to venture into the most challenging and radical rivers. And also for those who have no or little experience, you can come with your family and friends to have an innovative experience. We offer training and instruction, with trained and qualified guides and all the necessary safety equipment, on the calmest rivers and enjoying excellent tourist attractions.
